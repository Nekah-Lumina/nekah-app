# NEKAH

NEKAH is a maternal and infant health platform designed to support safer pregnancies and improve early-life healthcare outcomes through intelligent digital tools.

The platform assists mothers, healthcare providers, and health systems by providing tools for maternal health monitoring, early risk detection, and improved continuity of care.

## Key Features

- Maternal health tracking
- AI-supported symptom guidance
- Emergency referral support
- Baby development tracking
- Vaccination and health milestone records
- Healthcare provider connectivity

## System Vision

NEKAH is designed to integrate with secure digital health infrastructure such as BirthLedger, a blockchain-based verification layer for maternal and child health events.

Together, these systems aim to strengthen digital health records for mothers and children.

## Organization

Developed by NekahLumina Ventures.

## Relationship to BirthLedger

NEKAH is designed to integrate with BirthLedger, a blockchain-based verification layer for maternal and child health events.

BirthLedger provides tamper-resistant verification of important healthcare events such as births, antenatal visits, and vaccinations while NEKAH provides the user-facing maternal health platform.
