# nekah-app
NEKAH – AI-powered maternal and infant health platform designed to improve early risk detection, healthcare access, and continuity of care for mothers and children.

## Relationship to BirthLedger

NEKAH is designed to integrate with BirthLedger, a blockchain-based verification layer for maternal and child health events.

BirthLedger provides tamper-resistant verification of important healthcare events such as births, antenatal visits, and vaccinations while NEKAH provides the user-facing maternal health platform.
